Our Story

Leapfrog Software Technology Africa PLC is a young company that started three years ago. Our goal is to bridge the technological gap between traditional services and the needs of the public. Founded and owned by African entrepreneurs, we are committed to innovating and advancing industries through cutting-edge solutions.

Last year, we distributed over 137,000 SIM cards, demonstrating our robust telecom distribution network. We also launched the EZIBUS Ticketing Platform, revolutionizing ticketing for regional transportation companies.

We focus on creating "EZI products" that simplify complex processes. We've worked on transportation, specifically regional bus seat reservation, using EZIBUS. Now, we're getting ready to introduce ERP services powered by Odoo, which will help businesses streamline their operations.
